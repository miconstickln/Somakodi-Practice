About us

The Anyango's created an innovative, high quality, engaging, informal eating experience in a friendly neighborhood restaurant. Pizza King became a popular place with the teenage and college crowd. Pizza King attracted families that enjoyed a night out eating a meal that they would not be having at home. After the families left, Pizza King transformed itself into a hangout for college-age kids. Having a good time was an essential part of Pizza King.

Our Menu

Our menu contains a variety of pizza options, ranging from traditional pizzas such as Margherita and Pepperoni to specialty pizzas or gourmet recipes. Additionally, our pizza menu includes: appetizers such as garlic knots or cheese sticks, salads, sandwiches, and desserts.
